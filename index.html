Our Commitment

Beyond great food - our responsibility to our community

100% Halal Certified

All our meat is sourced from certified halal suppliers and prepared according to Islamic dietary laws. We maintain strict standards and regular audits to ensure compliance.

Our certification is through the Halal Monitoring Committee - UK, guaranteeing the highest standards of halal food preparation.

  • Certified halal meat suppliers
  • Separate preparation areas
  • Regular third-party audits
  • Staff trained in halal standards
  • Locally sourced vegetables when possible

Environmental Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices in every aspect of our operations.

From sourcing ingredients locally to minimizing waste, we believe in being stewards of the environment for future generations.

  • Compostable takeout containers
  • Energy-efficient kitchen equipment
  • Waste reduction program
  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • Eco-friendly packaging
